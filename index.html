What's in The UDAYAN 2K23

UDAYAN is the name chosen for the Technical Festival (TechFest) in Trident campus. This is completely separate from TRIFEST and is celebrated much ahead of the TRIFEST and is open to all other colleges of Odisha.
TECH VIRUS
The primary objective of celebrating “UDAYAN” in the campus is to offer a platform for display of the potentials of Trident students in the technology, innovation leadership and organization.
